================================================================================
INTERPRETING THE RESULTS
================================================================================

What is "Node Purity"?
  ‚Üí Fraction of the most common subject in each topological node
  ‚Üí Ranges from 0 (random mix) to 1.0 (perfect separation)
  ‚Üí Measures how well fingerprints separate topologically

Why isn't purity 100% everywhere?
  ‚Üí Overlapping cubes in Mapper intentionally mix samples
  ‚Üí This captures global topology (why Mapper is better than just DBSCAN)
  ‚Üí High-purity nodes still exist for authentication

Why are there 25 nodes for 80 samples?
  ‚Üí Overlapping cubes create redundancy
  ‚Üí Samples appear in multiple nodes
  ‚Üí Captures topological structure (not just partitioning)

What does "contamination" mean?
  ‚Üí Percentage of nodes mixing multiple subjects
  ‚Üí 84% means most regions aren't 100% pure
  ‚Üí But 24% high-confidence nodes are sufficient for auth
